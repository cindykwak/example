EVERYONE HAS A DREAM
The movie "Inception" deals with an incident that takes place when the main character infiltrates other people's dreams and steals their thoughts. The main character was able to deal with dreams in his dreams and rides in three dreams during running time.


The dreams overlap step by step. This look is similar to the characteristics of the Internet. To find the desired document, type in a search term and click a button. If the desired information does not come out from the page you accessed, it digs deeper and the Internet windows are often overlapped.


And the token of the main character to distinguish between dreams and reality was "spinning." If you spin the top and don't fall down, this place is a dream. Like you can only use a mouse and keyboard on the screen.
