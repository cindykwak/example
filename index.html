INCEPTION

Special Agent Cobb, an internationally wanted criminal, lives in hiding by infiltrating other people's dreams and stealing their thoughts. His new client, Saito, requests Operation Inception, which involves injecting thoughts rather than stealing thoughts, and makes an irresistible offer to launder Cobb's identity so he can escape his nomadic life and meet his family again. Cobb assembles the strongest team and executes Operation Inception, which has never been successful before.
